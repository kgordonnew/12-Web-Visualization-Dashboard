Summary: Latitude vs Longitude

This project included analysis of weather in over 700 cities across the world. The visualization included a series of scatter plots to showcase the relationship between Temperature (F) vs. Latitude; Humidity (%) vs. Latitude; Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude; and Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude. The analysis included Python-Pandas, OpenWeatherMap API, JSON traversals, Jupyter Notebook and Matplotlib library.
